Oliver Moya Bueno

About

Oliver Moya Bueno is a composer and producer based in Malaga, Spain.

Orchestral music specialist, he has composed more than one hundred original themes for video games in many different styles. His music is inspired by a great variety of artists like John Williams or Nobuo Uematsu.

In addition to his work as a musical creator, He is clarinetist and founder of Hey, Listen!, a musical trio dedicated mainly to the dissemination of video game music.

Featured

  • Breaking Fast (2017): composer and sound designer. Video game for sale on Steam.
  • Dark Ocean (2011): composer and sound designer. Video game Greenlit by the Steam community.

Awards

  • Burguer Master: composer and sound designer. Best Sound Award (GranadaJam 2017).
  • Panspermia: composer and sound designer. Best Sound Award (MalagaJam 2015).
